Construct Machine Learning: Create a Chatbot!

Hello, welcome to Construct ML :)
In this app, you can personalize and create your own ChatGPT AI chatbot!
Make sure to try to set personality traits of the chatbot,
such as how smart it is, how it speaks to you, and the way it acts.
Ask it questions and chat with it.
The goal is to understand how your chatbot will process data.
Have fun and learn AI!
